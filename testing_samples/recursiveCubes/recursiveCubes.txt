#####################################################################################################################################################################

* Inputs : 

- Starting cuboid : the root element that would be the base for the other cubes .
- Stopping volume : the volume at which the iterations are goin to stop at .

* Outputs :

- List of cuboids .

* Description : 

Using recursion to create new cubes on each face of the cuboid and repeating this
for each given cube until the cubes reach below or equal the stopping volume .
