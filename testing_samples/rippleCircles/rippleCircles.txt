* Inputs : 

- First Circle: the root cirlce which its center point is used 
		 for random translation .
- Stopping radius : the radius at which the iterations are goin to stop at .
- Random range : the range of random geometry translation for the root circle .

* Outputs :

- List of circles .

* Description : 

Creates a ripple like geometry by creating circles starting from a root circle which it scales down until it
stops replicating at a certain radius.
